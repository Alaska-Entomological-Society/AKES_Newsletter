\hypertarget{abstract}{%
\subsection{Abstract}\label{abstract}}

During the last decade three occurrence records for three species of
\emph{Nicrophorus -- N. guttula,} from Sitka Alaska, USA\emph{,}
\emph{N. hebes} from Alaska, and \emph{N. hybridus} from Whitehorse,
Yukon, Canada -- were published, which are likely erroneous because no
reliable evidence suggests these species occur or occurred in these
regions. This short note provides details, comments, and species
distribution modeling analyses on these records and concludes all three
are erroneous.
